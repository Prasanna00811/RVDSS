🏥 Health Services

💡 Daily Health Tips

  • Drink at least 3–4 liters of water daily
  • Eat fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise regularly for 30 minutes
  • Maintain personal hygiene

⚠️ Disease Awareness

  • Fever – Take rest and stay hydrated
  • Cold & Cough – Use warm fluids
  • Diarrhea – Drink ORS solution
  • Malaria – Use mosquito nets

🩺 First Aid Tips

  • Burns – Apply cold water immediately
  • Cuts – Clean and cover with bandage
  • Fainting – Lay person flat and elevate legs
